Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this app is to be a simple blackjack game that allows players to play against a computer. There will be a score and the computer will make bets based on simple statistical odds.

Audience

The audience for this app is primarily my peers, so other students at BYU-I.

Dynamic elements

The deck of cards will be stored as an array, and we will randomly select cards from it. There will be a function to remove shown cards from the array, as well as a function to shuffle the deck.

Simple "You Win" or "You Lose" modal, as well a a simple "How to Play" modal

How to play:

1. Choose a chip value and click to set your wager before each round begins. You can bet as little as $1 or go all-in.

2. Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. The dealer must hit on 16 and stand on 17.

3. Hit to draw another card. Stand to keep your hand.
